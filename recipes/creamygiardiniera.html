Creamy Giardiniera Dip

Description

We can all but guarantee this four-ingredient dip will become a party staple in your house. For the right final consistency and rich flavor, use oil-based giardiniera (also called Chicago-style) rather than a vinegar-based variety, which allows the giardiniera to seamlessly blend into the silky cream cheese, sour cream, and Parmesan mixture.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. Cream Cheese, Room Temperature
  • 1 oz. Parmesan, Finely Grated (about 1/2 cup)
  • 1/2 Cup Sour Cream
  • Kosher Salt
  • Freshly Ground Pepper
  • 1 Cup Drained Coarsley Chopped Oil-Based Giardiniera, Plus More for Serving and Oil from the Jar for Drizzling
  • Triscuits or Other Crackers (for serving)

Preparation

  1. Process 8 oz. cream cheese, room temperature, 1 oz. Parmesan, finely grated (about ½ cup), and ½ cup sour cream in a food processor until combined and smooth; season with kosher salt and freshly ground pepper. Add 1 cup drained coarsely chopped oil-based giardiniera and pulse until incorporated but dip is not completely smooth.
  2. Transfer dip to a small bowl. Top with chopped giardiniera and drizzle some oil from jar over. Serve with Triscuits or other crackers. Do Ahead: Dip (without toppings) can be made 1 day ahead. Cover and chill.
